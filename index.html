Welcome to OSCCW

The Odisha State Council for Child Welfare was established in the year 1959 under the provisions of the Society Registration Act, 1860. It is affiliated to the Indian Council for Child Welfare. In 1992, its bye laws were amended. At the district level, the District Councils for Child Welfare are supporting the State Council’s programmes.
